# 🥷 Daily_Ninja

Daily_Ninja is a **productivity app** designed to help you stay consistent, track your progress, and build powerful habits.  
It combines **visual motivation** with **routine alerts** so you can focus on what truly matters.

---

## ✨ Features

- 📊 **Heatmap Tracker**  
  Visualize your productivity streaks with a GitHub-style heatmap.  
  See your daily activity at a glance and identify patterns in your routine.

- ⏰ **Activity Column Alerts**  
  Get notified about tasks and routines you set.  
  Stay on track with gentle reminders that keep you moving forward.

- 🔥 **Streak Counter**  
  Track how many days in a row you’ve stayed consistent.  
  Build momentum and never break the chain.

---

## 🚀 Why Daily_Ninja?

- Encourages **habit formation** through streaks.  
- Provides **visual motivation** with heatmaps.  
- Keeps you **accountable** with activity alerts.  
- Simple, clean, and **reviewer-friendly design** for everyday use.
